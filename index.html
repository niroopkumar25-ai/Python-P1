Automated Attendance Management and Alert System (AAMAS)

  • Upload students
  • Record absence
  • Generate report
  • Blast alerts (email/SMS)

Manage attendance, compute absenteeism, and send automated alerts.

  • Upload students: Import a CSV with student details.
  • Record absence: Add weekly/class-hour absences.
  • Generate report: Auto-calculate percentages.
  • Alerts: Email alerts at 7%, 10%, 15%; warn at 20%.
